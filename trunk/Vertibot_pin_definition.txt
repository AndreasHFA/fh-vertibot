Vertibot Pin definition

GPIOA:	Pin4, (Pin5, Pin6)		CurB	
	Pin2, Pin3 			UART RX/TX
	Pin5, Pin6, Pin7		SPI (onboard Gyro)	
GPIOE:	Pin3				SPI (onboard Gyro)
GPIOB:	Pin0, Pin1			Enable MotA, Enable MotB	
	Pin6, Pin7			I2C (onboard Acc, Magnetometer)
	Pin9				XBeeRst
	Pin10, Pin11			XBee Txt / XBee Rxt UART 3
GPIOC:	Pin4, Pin5			CurA, Vbat	
	Pin6, Pin7, Pin8, Pin9		4 Channel Mot PWM Out
GPIOD:	Pin3, Pin4, Pin6, Pin7		4 Channel Mot PWM Out
	Pin12, Pin13, Pin14, Pin15	EXTI (4 Channel Receiver Input)
